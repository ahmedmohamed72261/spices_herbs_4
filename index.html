FAQs

Common Questions

  • 1. Do you export globally?

    Yes, Kingdom Spices Herbs exports to Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Middle East with all necessary certifications and documentation.

  • 2. Are your herbs organic?

    Most of our products are organically grown and certified. We also provide conventional herbs based on customer requirements.

  • 3. What herbs and spices do you offer?

    We supply basil, peppermint, thyme, oregano, fennel, anise, chamomile, hibiscus, and many more — all sustainably cultivated.

  • 4. Can you handle bulk orders?

    Absolutely. We work with large distributors and wholesalers, offering flexible packaging and shipping solutions.
